Eggs and Bagels Recipe

The God Father of Breakfast

Here is a recipe that is note passed down from anyone but is of my own creation. It's simple and it is nothing new but I have been perfecting the way I make eggs and bagels for a few years now. I eat them almost everyday for breakfast and I will show you how to make a fast and nutrious breakfast.

The Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 3 tablespoons of cream cheese
  • 1 tablespoon of unsalted butter
  • 1 whole bagel
  • 1/4 of a diced onion
  • 3 chopped mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup of cheddar cheese
  • 1 teaspoon Tapatio sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Black pepper
  1. You should start by cutting up the veggies. Dice the onion and chop the mushrooms into little bits.
  2. Start warming up a small pan and slice your bagel in half. Throw the bagel in the toaster oven for about 2 and a half minutes or the toaster itself.
  3. Next put your butter on the pan and when it is melted add your veggies.
  4. Stir the veggies and let them cook on low-medium heat for a few minutes.
  5. Crack your eggs into the pan when your onions and mushrooms look carmalized and up the heat to medium.
  6. Stir them to scramble the eggs and about a minute in add your pepper. About another minute in add your cheddar cheese and turn the heat of 30 seconds afterwards
  7. Your bagels should be nice and toasted and you should put your cream cheese on it.
  8. Lastly add you eggs onto your bagels and put a little bit of topatio on top for a little kick. Enjoy your nutrious breakfast!
